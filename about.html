MOTIF 54 is a capital intelligence studio focused on Africa's next strategic stack.

MOTIF 54 helps investors, institutions, and strategic operators understand where African value is forming across energy, minerals, compute, AI, sovereignty, and capital formation.

Founder

Sid Mofya is the Founder of MOTIF 54.

He previously served as CEO and Director of Draper Venture Network, a global alliance of venture capital firms, and as a Process Technologist with Royal Dutch Shell. He is a Kauffman Fellow and chemical engineer by training, with experience across energy, venture capital, African innovation ecosystems, and strategic capital formation.

His current work focuses on Africa's energy-minerals-compute stack, sovereign infrastructure, durable capital, and the systems through which African value is captured or lost.

Current focus

MOTIF 54 currently focuses on:

  • Energy-minerals-compute stack
  • Sovereign compute
  • Critical minerals
  • African capital formation
  • Durable capital
  • Strategic intelligence
