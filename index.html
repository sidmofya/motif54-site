The problem

Why capital misses African opportunities.

The challenge is rarely a lack of opportunity. The challenge is legibility.

Many projects struggle to attract capital because investors face:

  • Incomplete information
  • Unclear ownership structures
  • Weak investment narratives
  • Poor-quality diligence materials
  • Limited trusted local context

As a result, promising opportunities are overlooked while capital remains on the sidelines. MOTIF 54 exists to reduce uncertainty and improve decision quality.
