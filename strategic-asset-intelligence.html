Strategic asset intelligence

The intelligence behind a gate diagnostic.

Strategic asset intelligence is how MOTIF 54 reads a deal before committing to close its equity gate. It supports the work on this site, and does not stand apart from it.

Request a Gate Diagnostic
What it feeds

Every gate diagnostic starts here.

We assemble what is known about the asset, its ownership, its regulatory standing, and the capital already around the deal, then test that picture against what closing the equity gate would require.

What it covers
  • Ownership and control of the asset
  • Regulatory standing, rights, and permissions
  • The debt already interested and the conditions attached to it
  • The first-loss equity layer the deal still needs
  • The position of the local and sovereign owner
  • The timeline on which the gate could realistically close
How we use it

The intelligence produces a working view, held close to the deal. It informs whether the gate can close, what closing it requires, and on what timeline. Where the picture is incomplete, we say so plainly.

The fastest way to put it to work is a gate diagnostic on a specific deal.
