We invest and advise across three sectors where African ownership is structurally underrepresented and where long-horizon capital can compound meaningful advantage.

NATURAL RESOURCES

We back early-stage resource projects — minerals, energy, and land — in African jurisdictions where ownership structure and technical discipline are aligned. The value in African natural resources has historically transferred out of the continent at the structuring phase. We work to change that.

DATA INFRASTRUCTURE

African data infrastructure is under-owned by African capital. We invest in and support ventures building the physical and software layer that connects African institutions, markets, and people — with ownership structures that keep value on the continent.
